# HR Management System (HRMS) Web App - Page Structure

## 1. Authentication Pages
- **Login Page** – Employee & Admin sign-in (Google SSO, email/password).
- **Register Page** – New employee account creation (if allowed).
- **Forgot Password Page** – Reset password via email.

## 2. Dashboard (Main Page after Login)
- Overview of key HR metrics (employee count, leave requests, upcoming birthdays, etc.).
- Quick access to important features like leave requests, payroll, and announcements.

## 3. Employee Management
- **Employee List Page** – View all employees with search & filters.
- **Employee Profile Page** – Personal details, job role, contact info, and documents.
- **Add/Edit Employee Page** – HR/admin can create or update employee records.

## 4. Attendance & Leave Management
- **Attendance Tracking Page** – Clock-in/clock-out system with work hours tracking.
- **Leave Requests Page** – Employees can apply for leave, managers can approve/reject.
- **Leave Balance Page** – Shows available leave days for each employee.

## 5. Payroll & Salary Management
- **Salary Records Page** – View salary history, payslips, and deductions.
- **Payroll Processing Page** – HR can calculate & process employee salaries.
- **Tax & Deductions Page** – Displays tax breakdown, insurance, and benefits.

## 6. Performance & Reviews
- **Performance Review Page** – Managers can evaluate employee performance.
- **Goal Tracking Page** – Employees & managers can set and track performance goals.
- **Feedback Page** – Employees can receive feedback and improvement suggestions.

## 7. Documents & Compliance
- **Document Upload & Storage Page** – Stores employee contracts, IDs, certifications.
- **Company Policies Page** – Employees can read HR policies, terms, and guidelines.
- **Compliance Tracking Page** – HR can manage legal requirements & expirations.

## 8. Reports & Analytics
- **Employee Reports Page** – HR can generate reports on hiring, turnover, and attendance.
- **Salary & Payroll Reports Page** – Financial overview and salary distribution insights.
- **Leave & Absence Reports Page** – Tracks absenteeism trends.

## 9. Notifications & Announcements
- **Company Announcements Page** – HR can post news, events, and policy updates.
- **Employee Notifications Page** – Alerts for approvals, upcoming reviews, payroll, etc.

## 10. Settings & User Management
- **Profile Settings Page** – Employees can update personal information.
- **Role & Permissions Page** – HR can manage access levels (Admin, Manager, Employee).
- **System Settings Page** – Configure company-wide HRMS preferences.
